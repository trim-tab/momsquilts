Janine's Quilt

Janine's quilt was made for Janine using triangles. The triangles are put together in a way that the triangles line up. There were three other versions of how the triangles would line up. The triangles line up to become squares this is how that works together to become a quilt. No actual quilting knowledge was used in the writing of this summary.
